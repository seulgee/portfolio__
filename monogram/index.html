Monogram for Richard Knerr
2017

I designed a monogram for Richard Knerr, Co-Founder of Wham-O, who was known for his innovative marketing of the Hula Hoop and Frisbee in the United States. I also designed a business card for the monogram to be used for. I drew inspiration from Knerr's positive and playful personality, as well as from diagrams of the motion of the Hula Hoop.

The monogram in black & white, in larger and smaller scale.

Adobe Illustrator was used to create this project.
