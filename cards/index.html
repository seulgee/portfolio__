Greeting Cards
2018

In this project, I designed five separate greeting cards. Each card has a unique occasion associated with it. In order of appearance, the occasion goes: "Valentine's Day," "New Baby," "Get Well," "Thank You," and "Mother's Day."

Adobe Photoshop and Adobe InDesign were used to create this project.
