Dinner In Seoul
2018

I designed a dinner set collection with Seoul as the motif. As someone who was born and raised in Seoul for most of her life, I constantly find the city inspiring and evolving. While walking around Seoul, one can find oneself in starkly different environments because Seoul is a city that embodies and presents history and technology simultaneously. Through this dinner set, I wanted to create an experience that can imitate the fascinating and surreal harmony of these two elements.

Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator were used to create this project.
